==================================================
ACTORS & USE CASE DESCRIPTIONS
==================================================

1. ACTOR: SYSTEM ADMINISTRATOR
   - **Role**: The "Super User" of the SaaS platform.
   - **Responsibilities**:
     - Manages pharmacy registrations (Tenants) and subscription statuses.
     - Maintains the Global Medicine Catalog (Central Database).

2. ACTOR: PHARMACY OWNER
   - **Role**: The tenant owner who manages their specific pharmacy chain or store.
   - **Responsibilities**:
     - Has full control over their own pharmacyâ€™s data.
     - Manages staff accounts (Pharmacist/Cashier).
     - Configures branch settings and views financial reports (Revenue, P&L).

3. ACTOR: STAFF (Pharmacist/Cashier)
   - **Role**: Employees assigned to a specific pharmacy branch.
   - **Responsibilities**:
     - Perform daily operations: Inventory batching (stock in).
     - Process sales (POS) and handle cash/QR payments.
     - Customer record management (CRM) within their assigned branch.

4. ACTOR: CUSTOMER
   - **Role**: The end-user using the Mobile Application.
   - **Responsibilities**:
     - Manages personal health records.
     - Orders medications via the app.
     - Uses AI consultation (Future Feature).

5. ACTOR: PHARMA SALES REP (External)
   - **Role**: External suppliers (Data Entity).
   - **Note**: Currently interacts via "Email Bridge" rather than direct login.
   - **Responsibilities**:
     - Receives Purchase Requests from Owners via email.
     - Provides catalog updates (processed by System Admin).
